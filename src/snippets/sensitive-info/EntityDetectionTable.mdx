For the Rampart backend, **Model** means the type is detected by the on-device
NER model, and **Recognizer** means it is detected by a deterministic, validated
pattern (mirroring Rampart's deterministic redaction layer). Where the model and
a recognizer overlap on the same text, the recognizer wins.
