With Arcjet Email validation you can validate and verify email addresses.

Email validation is useful to reduce the amount of spam and fraudulent accounts
on your platform.

With other packages you can validate that email addresses are syntactically
correct too.
With Arcjet you can also verify that email addresses are disposable,
cannot receive email (no MX records),
and more,
without needing to set up any infrastructure.

## When to use

You can use Email validation to reduce spam or fraudulent accounts.

You can combine this with [Arcjet Rate limiting][arcjet-rate-limiting] to
prevent brute force attacks
or [Arcjet Sensitive info][arcjet-sensitive-info] to block unwanted data.

## How it works

Arcjet validates the email address syntax.

These algorithms are shipped locally within the WebAssembly bundled with SDKs.
This code is like the [`johnstonskj/rust-email_address`][github-email-address]
crate.

The Arcjet Cloud API verifies addresses that are syntactically valid.
This includes:

- **MX check**
